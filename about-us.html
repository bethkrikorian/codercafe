ABOUT US


Our Story

Coder Café was founded in October of 2019 at our location next to Tufts University in Somerville. This up-and-coming area bustles with college students looking for their daily dose of caffeine.


We founded Coder Café as four computer science students looking for a nice study atmosphere to claim as our own. Our vision was to provide Tufts students an environment that meets all their needs. Whether you’re a student trying to pull an all-nighter or start buzzing at the crack of dawn, a coder looking for a place to immerse yourself in your work and find a place to feel at home, or just another person looking for good coffee, we have you covered! We strive to create your ideal atmosphere, great opportunities for growth as a coder, and even better coffee!


Everything we do is for you - our menu, high quality products, location, atmosphere, and events.


Enjoy!

Becca, Beth, Dogacan, and Erce
